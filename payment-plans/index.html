Financing Options

Making Advanced Care Accessible

At Innovations Wellness & MedSpa, we understand that investing in your health and confidence is a meaningful decision. That’s why we offer flexible payment solutions that make it easier to say yes to the care you deserve.

CareCredit®

Healthcare financing with promotional no-interest periods. Apply online or in-office and receive an instant credit decision. Accepted for all services including wellness, injectables, laser, and skincare.

Cherry Financing

Quick, flexible financing with manageable monthly payment plans. No hard credit pull to check your options. Designed specifically for aesthetic and wellness treatments.

Treatment Packages

Ask about bundled treatment packages to save when committing to a series. Ideal for microneedling, IV therapy, laser series, and combination treatment plans.

In-Office Plans

Speak with our team about customized in-office arrangements for larger treatment plans or ongoing wellness programs. We will work with you.
