PCDC Lipolysis

PCDC
Lipolysis

Non-surgical fat reduction via phosphatidylcholine injections — targeting double chin, bra-line fat, and stubborn pockets permanently, without surgery or significant downtime.

Struggling with a stubborn double chin that won’t respond to diet or exercise? PCDC lipolysis offers a minimally invasive solution for targeted fat reduction — treating the double chin, bra-line back fat, and smaller areas of the stomach.

How PCDC Lipolysis Works

The treatment uses phosphatidylcholine deoxycholic acid, a compound naturally found in the body that breaks down dietary fat. When injected directly into targeted fat tissue, it permanently destroys fat cells, which your body then naturally eliminates over 4–8 weeks. Fat cells that are destroyed will not return.

Benefits

  • Permanently reduces the double chin and improves jawline definition
  • 30–60 minute outpatient procedure performed in-office
  • Minimal recovery — most patients return to normal activities the same day
  • Long-lasting results — treated fat cells are permanently eliminated
  • Also treats bra-line back fat and small abdominal pockets

Are You a Candidate?

  • Stubborn fat beneath the chin that doesn’t respond to diet or exercise
  • Good overall health with realistic expectations
  • At or near your ideal body weight
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding

Most patients see significant results after 2–3 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart.
