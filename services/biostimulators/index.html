Radiesse® Biostimulator

Biostimulators —
Collagen From Within

Radiesse® is an advanced regenerative injectable that supports your skin’s natural collagen production — restoring firmer, smoother, more youthful-looking skin from within.

At Innovations Wellness & MedSpa, we offer Radiesse® Biostimulator, an advanced regenerative injectable designed to support your skin’s natural collagen production and restore a firmer, smoother, more refreshed appearance over time.

Rather than simply filling an area, Radiesse® works by stimulating your body’s own regenerative response — helping improve skin quality, support underlying structure, and address visible signs of aging in a beautifully natural way. It’s an ideal treatment for patients seeking subtle, elegant rejuvenation with long-term skin-enhancing benefits.

What Is Radiesse® Biostimulator?

Radiesse® is a collagen-stimulating injectable made with calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) microspheres — a biocompatible material that has been used safely in medicine for many years. When strategically placed beneath the skin, Radiesse® helps stimulate the body’s natural production of:

  • Collagen
  • Elastin
  • Dermal support

Over time, this regenerative process improves skin texture, firmness, elasticity, and overall skin quality.

How Radiesse® Works

Once injected, the CaHA particles act as a scaffold beneath the skin, encouraging your body’s own repair and collagen-building response. As this process unfolds, the skin gradually becomes:

  • Firmer
  • Smoother
  • More resilient
  • More youthful in appearance

Rather than creating an overfilled look, Radiesse® supports a more natural, refined rejuvenation by improving the quality and integrity of the tissue itself.

Benefits of Radiesse® Biostimulator

Collagen Stimulation

Supports your body’s natural collagen response — stronger, healthier-looking tissue with long-term rejuvenation from within.

Firmer, Smoother Skin

As collagen remodels, skin appears more lifted and refined — with improved firmness, elasticity, and reduced laxity.

Natural-Looking Rejuvenation

Elegant, subtle enhancement that improves skin quality without an artificial appearance — refined, never overdone.

Long-Lasting Results

Because results come from your own collagen production, they develop gradually and last well beyond many traditional treatments.

What Can Radiesse® Treat?

Radiesse® may be used to improve areas affected by collagen loss, laxity, and visible aging changes. Common treatment areas include:

  • Lower face
  • Jawline
  • Pre-jowl area
  • Chin
  • Neck
  • Hands
  • Select body areas with skin laxity or crepiness, depending on candidacy

Your treatment plan will be tailored to your anatomy, skin quality, and aesthetic goals.

Radiesse® stimulates your own collagen — supporting firmer, smoother, more beautiful skin the way nature intended.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Radiesse® Biostimulator may be an excellent option for patients noticing:

  • Skin laxity
  • Loss of firmness
  • Crepey skin
  • Early to moderate aging changes
  • Declining skin quality
  • Texture changes associated with collagen loss

It’s especially ideal for patients who want a treatment focused on skin rejuvenation and collagen support — rather than simply replacing volume.

What to Expect

Radiesse® treatments are performed in-office with minimal downtime. Most patients can return to normal activities shortly after treatment. You may experience:

  • Mild swelling
  • Temporary redness
  • Minor bruising at injection sites

These effects are typically short-lived and resolve within a few days. Results develop gradually as your body begins producing new collagen and remodeling the treated tissue over time.
