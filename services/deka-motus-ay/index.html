Deka Motus AY

Motus AY Laser —
Hair Removal & Skin Rejuvenation

Pain-free permanent hair reduction and skin rejuvenation with MOVEO technology — fast, comfortable, and safe for all skin types.

At Innovations Wellness & MedSpa, we’re proud to offer the advanced Deka Motus AY Laser — delivering fast, comfortable, and effective treatments for hair removal and select skin concerns, all with minimal discomfort and downtime.

What Makes Motus AY Different?

MOVEO Technology for Maximum Comfort

The Motus AY features innovative MOVEO technology, which uses continuous, in-motion energy delivery instead of traditional pulsed laser shots. This allows for:

  • Gradual heating of the hair follicle
  • Reduced risk to surrounding skin
  • A virtually pain-free experience

Combined with a cooled sapphire tip, treatments are safe, gentle, and well-tolerated for most patients.

Fast, Comfortable Treatments

Unlike traditional lasers that can feel like a rubber-band snap, the Motus AY delivers energy smoothly and evenly. Patients love that treatments are:

  • Quick — often just minutes per area
  • Comfortable with minimal sensation
  • Efficient, with excellent results

Safe for All Skin Types

One of the biggest advantages of the Motus AY is its ability to safely treat a wide range of skin tones. This means more patients qualify for treatment, with greater flexibility in care and effective results without compromising safety.

More Than Hair Removal

The Motus AY is more than just a hair removal laser — it can also improve overall skin appearance and help target select pigment and vascular concerns.

Permanent Hair Reduction

Pain-free, fast sessions that deliver long-lasting hair reduction on virtually every skin tone.

Red Carpet Glow Laser Facial

A radiant, pre-event refresh — smoother texture, brighter tone, and camera-ready luminosity.

Pigmented Lesions

Improves sun spots and discoloration — spots darken briefly, then flake and fade over 1–3 weeks.

Vascular Lesions

Helps reduce redness and small vessels — gentle warmth after treatment, gradual improvement over sessions.

Laser Facial / “Red Carpet Glow”

The Motus AY can also be used as a Laser Facial to help refresh the skin before a special event — weddings, parties, photoshoots, vacations, date nights, and other occasions.

  • Helps give skin a healthy, radiant glow
  • Improves the appearance of dull or tired-looking skin
  • Targets mild pigmentation and redness
  • Supports a smoother, brighter overall complexion
  • Ideal as a pre-event “Red Carpet Glow” treatment

Pigmented Lesions (Sun Spots & Discoloration)

After treatment, pigmented spots may temporarily darken before gradually flaking and fading over 1–3 weeks.

Vascular Lesions (Redness & Small Vessels)

For vascular concerns, mild redness or warmth may occur after treatment, and areas typically improve gradually. Multiple sessions may be recommended for best results.

What to Expect After Treatment

You may experience mild redness, warmth, or slight swelling. These effects typically resolve within hours to a few days.

Aftercare Guidelines

  • Avoid direct sun exposure
  • Use daily SPF
  • Avoid heat (saunas, hot tubs, intense workouts) for 24–48 hours if advised
  • Do not pick or exfoliate treated areas
  • Follow all provider-specific instructions

How Many Treatments Will I Need?

Most patients require a series of treatments for optimal results. Your customized plan depends on hair thickness and color, skin type, treatment area, and desired outcome.
